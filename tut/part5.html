Let's Make a Canvas Library, Part 5: Input

This page includes a stripped-down demo of the InputManager module, in the form of a simple "Snake" game (minus the stuff that requires collision detection — that comes later).

I've set InputManager up with several different control schemes for the snake:

    Touchscreen devices:
  • swipe to change direction
  • single-tap on top/bottom/left/right of canvas to move in that direction
  • two-fingered tap to stop
    Keyboard:
  • Arrow keys, Numeric keypad arrows, and IJKL keys to change direction
  • P or SPACE to Panic, Q or ESC to stop moving

For a more detailed discussion of what InputManager is doing behind the scenes, read the related blog post.
